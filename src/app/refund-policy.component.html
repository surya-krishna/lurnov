Refund Policy

Thank you for subscribing to Lurnov. Please read our refund policy carefully before making any purchase.

Strict No Refund Policy

All sales are final. Due to the digital nature of our products and the immediate access granted to our premium features and AI content upon purchase, we do not offer refunds, returns, or exchanges for any subscription plans or one-time purchases.

Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Upon cancellation:

  • Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
  • No pro-rated refunds will be issued for the remaining unused portion of your subscription term.

Free Trials

If you signed up for a free trial, you must cancel before the trial period ends to avoid being charged. Once the trial ends and the payment is processed, the standard No Refund Policy applies.

Technical Issues

We are committed to providing a high-quality service. If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using the Service, please contact our support team immediately. However, the existence of a technical issue does not automatically entitle you to a refund. We reserve the right to attempt to resolve the issue first.

Exceptions

Refunds are granted solely at the absolute discretion of Lurnov management and only in exceptional circumstances (e.g., duplicate charges due to a system error). Any such refund does not constitute a waiver of this policy for future instances.
